Nachos

Nachos are a Tex-Mex culinary dish consisting of tortilla chips or totopos covered with cheese or cheese sauce, as well as a variety of other toppings and garnishes, often including meats, vegetables, and condiments such as salsa, guacamole, or sour cream.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound ground beef (80:20 lean-to-fat ratio)
  • 16 ounces (2 cups) refried beans, canned or homemade
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 large bag of tortilla chips
  • 4 ounces cheddar cheese, grated (about 2 cups), plus more for topping
  • 4 ounces Colby Jack cheese, grated (about 2 cups), plus more for topping
  • 1 cup pico de gallo, store-bought or homemade, plus more for topping
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 jalapeño (pickled or fresh), sliced

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F
  2. Make the taco spice blend
  3. Make the beef and bean topping
  4. Toast the chips
  5. Assemble and bake the nachos
  6. Top and serve
